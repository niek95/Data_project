Usage

The map of New york can be manipulated with the sliders seen below. Either a specific time of day, or a specific day of the week can be selected. Additionally, a crime type can be selected in the dropdown menu. Data will be shown for either violations, misdemeanors or felonies. When a precinct in the map is clicked, data specific to that precinct will be shown in the line graph and calendar.
