Police budgets are never big enough to handle all cases being reported. To make sure the available budget is being spent as efficiently as possible it is useful to know when and where certain types of crimes are committed. This way, police departments can allocate resources where they are needed. As the amount of crimes is very high for a city as big as New York, I built an interactive visualization to help police departments in determining where and when capacity is needed the most.

Data source: https://www.kaggle.com/adamschroeder/crimes-new-york-city
